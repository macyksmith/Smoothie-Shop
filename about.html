Smooth Vibes Smoothie Bar

Our Story

Smooth Vibes started as a late-night idea in a campus library — what if “energy” didn’t have to mean vending-machine snacks or burnt coffee? We wanted something bright, fresh, and actually fun to drink.

Today, Smooth Vibes is built for students who sprint between classes, jobs, and study sessions. Every drink is customizable, colorful, and crafted to boost your day.

How It Works

  • 1. Pick your base: fruity, green, or protein-packed.
  • 2. Choose your liquid: dairy, oat, almond, or coconut milk.
  • 3. Add boosts: espresso, matcha, protein, collagen, vitamin C.
  • 4. Order ahead: place your order online and grab it between classes.

Whether you follow our signature blends or build your own, you’ll leave with something delicious and energizing.

Why Students Love Us

We’re designed around student life — extended evening hours, fast pickup, and a menu that works for vegan, dairy-free, and high-protein diets.

We also rotate limited-time flavors inspired by finals week, game days, and seasonal fruit. Check the home page’s Flavor Wheel for fun ideas.
