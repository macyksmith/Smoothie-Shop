Smooth Vibes Smoothie Bar

Our Story

Smooth Vibes started as a late-night idea in a campus library: what if “energy” didn’t have to mean vending-machine sugar or another burnt coffee? We wanted something bright, fresh, and actually fun to drink.

Today, Smooth Vibes is designed for students who sprint between classes, jobs, and study sessions. Every drink is built to be customizable—swap milks, add protein, or throw in an espresso shot when the semester gets real.

How It Works

  • 1. Pick your base: fruit-forward, green, or protein-heavy.
  • 2. Choose your liquid: dairy, oat, almond, or coconut milk.
  • 3. Add boosts: espresso, matcha, protein, collagen, or vitamin C.
  • 4. Order ahead: place your order online and grab it between classes.

Our goal is to keep the menu simple but flexible. You can follow our signature recipes or build your own from scratch. Either way, you leave with something that tastes good and actually supports your day.

Why Students Love Us

We’re built around student schedules: extended evening hours, quick pickup, and a menu that works for vegan, dairy-free, and high-protein diets. Smooth Vibes is the spot you swing by before a presentation, after the gym, or on your way to a late-night study group.

We also rotate limited-time flavors inspired by finals week, game days, and seasonal fruit. Check the home page “Flavor Vibe Randomizer” for ideas and upcoming specials.
